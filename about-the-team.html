About the team

  • CASHe is an unsecured personal loan giving mobile app founded by Mr. V Raman Kumar in 2016
  • His aim is to bring millions of young salaried professionals who couldn’t gain credit from conventional banks
  • He also wanted to showcase that the conventional credit evaluation metrics were inadequate especially for young salaried professionals
  • For that reason, he launched CASHe to provide credit to those who didn’t quality for credit from conventional lending agencies
