About CASHe

  • CASHe is a loan giving mobile app for salaried professionals
  • It disburses short-term personal loan to people who don’t have credit history
  • Loans range from Rs.1,000 to Rs.4,00,000 with the credit period ranging from 3 months to 1 ½ year
  • Loan lending would be in quick time
  • The major benefit of CASHe is it doesn’t make the borrower approach for loan waiting for longer time physically

Social Loan Quotient

  • Credit score system that evaluates whether a customer is eligible for the loan
  • The score ranges from 0 to 995
  • The higher the score, the lower chances to get into defaulter state
  • This system distinguishes good borrowers from bad borrowers

Four easy steps to get the SLQ score

  • Download the CASHe app
  • Fill up the personal details
  • Submit KYC documents
  • Know your SLQ score in seconds
